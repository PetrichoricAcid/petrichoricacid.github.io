About Me

Hi! I'm Jakob Duquaine I'm a psychology major and computer science minor at St. Norbert College, De Pere, WI.

Hey, I'm your average college nerd. I'm the secretary of SNC's Computer Science Club and I am the president of the Strings n' Things crafting Club.

  • I love videogames, I've been playing Deadlock a lot recently (a lot...), with Balatro, KSP, and Project Zomboid intermixed. I used to be obsessed with Elden Ring and play CS2 all the time, CS:GO was better.
  • I do crafts occassionally in my free time, I enjoy plastic canvas and whatever other projects I'm given at Strings n' Things. My next project will be sewing Cheez-It crackers.
  • I love my dorm plants, I care for an aloe named Planty the first, two succulants, a pot of bachelors (cornflowers), and a mystery herb (I forgot what I planted....).
  • I've been having fun starting a homelab using a cheap ThinkPad I got from my high school, streaming whatever current anime I'm watching from Jellyfin and hosting a Minecraft server that hasn't quite been set up yet for local use. I also am exploring some coding projects, making my own Mahjong in CMake with C++ to somewhat learn GUI handling with SDL3.
